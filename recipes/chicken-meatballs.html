Baked Chicken Meatballs

Back to all recipes

Description

This recipe comes from Sally's Baking Recipes. These delicious baked chicken meatballs are super versatile. They go great w/ marinara sauce or on a meatball sub. The chicken base makes this recipe a really healthy option, and it's really easy to make them in bulk. The meatballs freeze well and can be stored in the fridge for up to 1 week.
